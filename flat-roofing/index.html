Frequently Asked Questions

Which type of flat roof is best?
EPDM rubber and GRP fibreglass are extremely durable, seamless and virtually maintenance-free with long guarantees, while felt remains a proven, cost-effective option. We'll recommend the best fit for your roof and budget.
How long does an EPDM or fibreglass flat roof last?
Well-installed EPDM and GRP flat roofs can last 25–30+ years, which is why they often carry longer guarantees than traditional felt.
Do you repair existing flat roofs?
Yes — we install, repair and maintain all types of flat roof on homes, extensions, garages, industrial units, shops and offices.

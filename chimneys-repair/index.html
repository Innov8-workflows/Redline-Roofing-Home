Frequently Asked Questions

Do you rebuild chimneys as well as repair them?
Yes — we handle everything from minor repointing and flashing repairs to complete chimney rebuilds, always to building-regulation standards.
What are the signs my chimney needs repair?
Crumbling mortar, leaning stacks, damaged flaunching or pots, damp patches on internal chimney breasts and failing lead flashing are all signs it's time for a chimney inspection.
Is chimney work safe and compliant?
Absolutely. We ensure proper ventilation, use quality materials and comply with building regulations on every chimney job, and we're fully insured.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know about working with Redline Roofing.

Do you offer free roofing quotes in Evesham?
Yes. All of our quotes are completely free and no-obligation. We'll visit your property, assess the work and provide a clear written quote — just call Freephone 0800 9330975 or request a callback.
Are Redline Roofing insured?
Yes, we are fully insured with £1,000,000 public liability cover, so you're protected on every job.
Do you guarantee your work?
We do. Our workmanship comes with a 15-year guarantee for complete peace of mind.
Which areas do you cover?
We're based in Rous Lench near Evesham and cover Evesham, Worcester, Redditch, Studley, Pershore, Broadway, Alcester, Droitwich and surrounding towns and villages across Worcestershire and South Warwickshire.
Do you take payment in cash?
No — we don't do cash jobs. You'll always receive a proper written quote and invoice.
How much does a new roof cost?
Every roof is different, so cost depends on size, pitch, materials and access. We provide a free, no-obligation written quote after assessing your roof — call Freephone 0800 9330975.
How long does a re-roof take?
A typical house re-roof usually takes a few days to around a week depending on the size of the roof and the weather. We'll give you a clear timescale with your quote.
Do you repair roofs as well as replace them?
Yes. Where a repair is the more sensible and cost-effective option, we'll always tell you honestly. We fix slipped and broken tiles, leaks, flashing, ridges and storm damage.
Which type of flat roof is best?
EPDM rubber and GRP fibreglass are extremely durable, seamless and virtually maintenance-free with long guarantees, while felt remains a proven, cost-effective option. We'll recommend the best fit for your roof and budget.
Do you fit fascias, soffits and guttering together?
Yes — fitting fascias, soffits, downpipes and guttering together gives the cleanest, most weatherproof finish, and we can do it all in one visit.
How often should gutters be cleaned?
For most homes, clearing gutters once or twice a year — especially in autumn — prevents blockages, overflow and damp. Homes near trees may need it more often.
Do you rebuild chimneys as well as repair them?
Yes — we handle everything from minor repointing and flashing repairs to complete chimney rebuilds, always to building-regulation standards.
Do you offer emergency or storm-damage repairs?
Yes. Storms can cause slipped tiles, leaks and damaged flashing — get in touch and we'll get to you as quickly as we can to make things watertight.
